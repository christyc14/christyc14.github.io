About

I'm a final year computer engineering student at Imperial College London. I have strong academic and sporting related achievements, most notably representing Great Britain in target rifle shooting and powerlifting.


Most recently, I was selected for the McKinsey Next Generation Women Leaders event, and Bain's True North Scholarship workshop for women. I am the first undergraduate president of Women in Computing at Imperial College, and the first incoming female president of ICBarbell Club.


I am an incoming associate consultant at Bain & Company.


I'm originally from Hong Kong, so am able to speak fluent English, semi-fluent Cantonese, and am working proficient in Mandarin.


I'd love to connect with people from all walks of life, so please don't hesitate to send me a message!
