Electronic Musical Instrument for those with mobility issues

I had the pleasure of meeting 家瑜 (Macy), back in 2014 when I was hospitalized for an extended period of time. She had mobility issues, which included the inability to walk without support from either a cane or walker, and limited use of one of her hands. She thoroughly enjoyed music and had tried numerous times to play a musical instrument. She was unable to due to her issues and this took a toll on her mental health.

Using my newfound Arduino skills at the time, I coded up an electronic musical instrument similar to the clarinet (without the need to blow), using capacitive touch buttons. I then created and tested various shapes and prototypes with her to see what suited her needs best.

Eventually we found a solution, and she was ecstatic to finally be able to make music. She still uses the instrument to this day.
